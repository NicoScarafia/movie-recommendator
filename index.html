Movie Recommender

🎬 The objective of this site is to recommend one or more films, based on the user's movie genre preferences. Once a genre is selected by the user, the recommended movies will be displayed on screen.

👌 The user can also view all the movies that are recommended - despite their genre - OR select the #RandomRecommendation option so that a random movie will be recommended to him/her.

🎮 Lastly, there's a #mini-quiz game to play!
